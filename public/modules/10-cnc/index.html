10.2 Shopbot Reservation

What am I Turning In? Take a Screenshot of your ShopBot Reservation Take a Screenshot of your Plywood Receipt Label the Screenshots Put them in a labeled folder Upload the Folder to Google Drive Google Drive Upload Folder https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1G6UE8S1b1FCGJx2BNRLiLxxd5dEi7xRl Project Documentation is due before the final critique, uploaded to Google Drive. Make sure you make your reservation soon enough to have enough time to assemble your project, apply finishing and take documentation photos.

10.3 Fusion 360 Model of CNC Project

What Do I Turn In? Fusion 360 File .f3d file with your model complete and manufacture workspace setup with toolpaths Rendering of CNC Project with appearances applied. Should be in a 4:3 2:3 1:1 or 16:9 aspect ration with one side at least 4000px Rendering of top view of parts laid flat. Show an interesting view of the parts laid flat. You will need to duplicate your model in the design workspace and arrange it flat.
