13 - 3D Animation

13.2 3d Animation Sets

Make an Animation Set(s) & Assets 1. Make a List of Needed Assets After you make your storyboards, make a list of the items and settings for your animation. Is there a boat and water? Is there a box of donuts? Does the ball sit on a shelf? Think of the simple shapes and polygons that can start to make these objects. A box of donuts could start with a scaled cube and a bunch of torus primatives in it, a shelf is just multiple scaled cubes, a boat is a bit more complex but is an elongated cube with some vetecies moved around.
